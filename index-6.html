About Binita Makeup

Welcome to Binita Makeup, Siliguri's trusted beauty brand founded by Binita Paswan with a simple mission — to make every woman feel confident, beautiful, and celebrated without breaking the bank.

"Beauty is for everyone. Our goal is to make every woman feel her absolute best — every single day."

— Binita Paswan, Founder
